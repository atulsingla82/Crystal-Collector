Crystals Collector!


Instructions:

You will be given a random number to start the game.

You have four crystals. Each crystal will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the winning number. You lose if your total score goes above the winning number.

The value of each crystal is revealed when you click on a specific one.

Every time a new game starts, the the value of each crystal changes.
