Crystals Collector!

Instructions:

You will be given a random number to start the game.

There are four crystals below. Each crystal will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. You lose if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each crystal is revealed when you click on a specific one.

Every time a new game starts, the the value of each crystal changes.
